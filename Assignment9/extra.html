Extra Tips

  • A magnetic screwdriver is useful if you have it, but it isn't necassery by any means.
  • The PS5 cover can be a pain to put back. I found that I had to put quite a bit of force into getting it to clip back in.
  • Make sure to keep track of the smaller screw and the spacer. The spacer is exceptionally prone to rolling away.
  • The SSD does require some force to fully connect, but as long as it is alligned correctly it should be ok.
